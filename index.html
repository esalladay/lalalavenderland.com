--- title: La La Lavenderland layout: default ---

We are a small family farm located in Spokane, WA where we grow and hand pick our all natural lavender with no chemicals or pesticides. With our unique blend of lavender and herbs, we create salves, lip balms, bath salts, sachets, bouquets, wreaths, and more! Gifts from our garden are hand made and fashioned in the cottage craft tradition.
